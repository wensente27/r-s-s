How it's Relevant

The UNSDGs played a big part in the forming of this project. From how we conduted the tests, doing our research and what have you!

Relevance

The project we are implementing is in coordination with UN SDGs 3, 6, 12, 13, 14, and 15. They are Good Health and Well-Being, Clean Water and Sanitation, Responsible Consumption and Production, Climate Action, Life Below Water, and Life on Land. They are connected with our website because proper waste disposal and segregation is a good way to reduce the effects of Climate Change since pollution is one of the major causes of climate change. It is also a good way to contribute to Good Health and Well-Being, Clean Water and Sanitation, Life Below Water, and Life on Land because it keeps our environment clean and healthy, making ourselves and the animals in turn healthier because a dirty and polluted environment can stunt our health and well-being. Responsible Consumption and Production is also connected to our website because we are also going to teach you the 3Rs, how to properly reduce, reuse and recycle to lessen the need to produce excessive amounts of goods.
